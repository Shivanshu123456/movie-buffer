Harry Potter Characters

Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a young wizard known for his bravery and the lightning-shaped scar on his forehead. He is famous for defeating the dark wizard Voldemort.

Hermione Granger

Hermione Granger is one of Harry Potter's best friends and known for her intelligence and resourcefulness. She is a top student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Ron Weasley

Ron Weasley is Harry Potter's other best friend and known for his loyalty and sense of humor. He comes from a large wizarding family.

Albus Dumbledore

Albus Dumbledore is the wise and powerful headmaster of Hogwarts. He is known for his fight against dark forces and his role as a mentor to Harry Potter.

Severus Snape

Severus Snape is a complex character, known for his role as Potions Master and later Headmaster at Hogwarts. He is revealed to have a deep, secret loyalty to Harry Potter.

Draco Malfoy

Draco Malfoy is a student at Hogwarts and rival to Harry Potter. He is known for his wealthy background and his antagonistic role throughout the series.

Rubeus Hagrid

Rubeus Hagrid is the Keeper of Keys and Grounds at Hogwarts. He is known for his love of magical creatures and his friendship with Harry Potter.

Ginny Weasley

Ginny Weasley is Ron's younger sister and Harry Potter's eventual love interest. She is known for her strong will and talent in magic.

Luna Lovegood

Luna Lovegood is a quirky and kind-hearted student at Hogwarts. She is known for her unique perspective on the magical world and her loyalty to her friends.

Bellatrix Lestrange

Bellatrix Lestrange is a fanatical Death Eater and one of Voldemort's most loyal followers. She is known for her cruelty and strong magical abilities.

Horace Slughorn

Horace Slughorn is the Potions Master who succeeds Severus Snape. He is known for his knack for recognizing talented students and his connections with influential wizards.

Minerva McGonagall

Minerva McGonagall is the Deputy Headmistress of Hogwarts and Head of Gryffindor House. She is known for her strict but fair approach to teaching and her transformation abilities.

Sirius Black

Sirius Black is Harry Potter's godfather and a member of the Order of the Phoenix. He is known for his rebellious nature and tragic fate.
